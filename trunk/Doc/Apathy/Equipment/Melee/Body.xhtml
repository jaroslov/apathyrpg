A Cestus has a similar function as Similar as brass knuckles, though is made in a different manner. A Cestus is a leather or fabric strap that is wrapped around the hand and tied at the wrist. Sap gloves are leather gloves which have lead or steel shot sewn into the back of the fingers and hand. This protects the wearer's hands in a fight, and the extra weight deals extra damage. They are commonly used by bouncers, security personnel, and police forces. Since the gloves are attached to the hand it is impossible for them to be dropped accidentally. It is possible to wear sap gloves while holding other items in the hand.

Acts as soft leather armor on the hands. Cannot be disarmed.
