A knife with a scalloped or otherwise serrated edge. The serrations are very effective for damaging flesh and also for cutting fibrous materials such as rope or cloth, but they reduce the knife's effectiveness at penetrating hard materials. Knives of this sort are commonly issued as "survival knives" and are also popular tools for miliary use. The serrated knife is much more useful as a tool than straight-edged knives.

Only deals 1D6 vs. Hard Armor
