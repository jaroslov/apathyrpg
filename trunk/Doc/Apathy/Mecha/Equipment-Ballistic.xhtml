Snub Cannon

Future

(Class+1)*10

(Class+1)*5

The snub cannon is a devastating short-range cannon which fires shells of unusually heavy weight. The snub cannon may be fired directly, like a rifle, from point-blank to a range of 100 meters. Beyond that point the shell droops too far to aim reliably. The snub cannon may also be fired in a high arc like a mortar with the aid of an Artillery Computer. Fired in this manner the range is 100-1000 meters and the shells fall straight down on the the target, reaching a very high terminal velocity which adds AP:20 and deals an additional 50% damage. The snub cannon has a short but very heavy barrel, massive heavyweight shells, and a much more powerful detonant than a normal cannon. Deals (Class+1)/2 (rounded up!) x20 C (Crushing) damage. The mecha may not fire while moving or the next action after firing. Recovery time is 3, reloading must be performed manually. Reloding requires dextrous limbs and hands, be they human or robotic.
