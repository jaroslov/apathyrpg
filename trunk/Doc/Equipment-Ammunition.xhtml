These are the most effective hunting arrows. They aren't great at penetrating heavy armor, but are excellent for causing additional damage to the target. This is particularly desirable for hunting, because it is likely to kill or incapacitate the target animal quickly, even if the vital organs are missed. A broadhead is a wide, diamond-shaped head that has two or more “fins” on it that are sharpened into blades—which cut a devastating wound in the target.
