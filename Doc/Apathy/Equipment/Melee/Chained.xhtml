This is a length of ordinary iron chain suitable for striking an opponent with--anywhere from 2 to 4 feet long. This can be either a traditional chain (such as that used for lifting loads or securing a gate), or a motorcycle drive chain. After a successful strike, the use may spend an action to trip the target, and deal the second damage listed. A chain cannot be used to parry. The character must have an appropriate Trip-related Martial Arts skill to use this sort of chain to trip.

The Flail was originally a grain-threshing tool, but has since been adapted for combat. A flail consists of a stout handle capped with a hinged (or shortly chained) iron head which is often studded or spiked. It resembles a ball-and-chain but has a longer handle, a larger striking head, and a shorter chain.
