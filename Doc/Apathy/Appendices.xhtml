Spending luck

What

How

Skills

Roll your full skill's worth of dice and bonus again, and add the number of successes to your previous roll. Can only be done once per roll.

Damage

Add +1 to damage roll per luck point spent.

Dodge

Add +5 to some dodge-related skill per luck point spent. If you don’t have a dodge-related skill, then you are assumed to have one die in Dodge. Alternately, get a 25% chance to dodge for each luck point spent, cumulatively.

Soak

For each luck point spent, reduce the damage by 2 points. Alternately, for each luck point spent, you get a 20% chance to take no damage; this accumulates.

Action

Take an extra action at any time.

Healing

Gain 5 Hit Points instantaneously.

Magic

Gain 50 Mana Points instantaneously.
