Arcane Talent

No value

Varies, see description

This is a stand in. A character may take a magical spell of any rank as a Trait. They do not need to buy Magical Control, Spellcasting, Faith, Mysticism, etc. The rank of the specific Trait is the rank of the spell. For purposes of control (striking, LVL, etc.), the level at which the Spell Ability has been purchased is used for these calculations. The cost of using the ability is the same Mana Point cost as the spell itself.

Ignaceous Charcot Flamme

Ignaceous’ alter-identity is the mutant superhero, Flame-On!The exclamation point is part of the name.. He has the following Arcane Talents: Fireblast at Level 4, Fire Shards at level 4, Flame Shroud at level 1, Living Fire at level 1, and Flame Whip at level 2. Although he feels it was unnecessary, his secret master has been forcing him to train in the “mystic arts” to learn to control his powers better (Mysticism, level 3). Although he thinks it’s bunk, and wasn’t necessary, he can certainly use his powers longer nowadays.

We note that for Flame Whip, Fire Shards, and Fireblast the description in says to “roll” to determine if Ignaceous strikes. Ignaceous would roll his level he has purchased the skills at, i.e. levels 2, 4, and 4, respectively.
